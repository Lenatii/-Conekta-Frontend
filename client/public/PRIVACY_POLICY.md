**Last Updated:** November 23, 2025  
**Effective Date:** November 23, 2025

## 1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to **CONEKTA** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services through WhatsApp (Mama Dennis chatbot) and our platform.

**CONEKTA** is a marketplace platform connecting tenants with landlords, and clients with service providers (fundis) in Nakuru, Kenya. We also provide identity verification services through our CONEKTA Trust system.

By using CONEKTA, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the **Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019**.

---

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register or use CONEKTA services, we collect:

**For All Users:**
- Full name
- Phone number (WhatsApp number)
- City and area (location)
- Profile information

**For Landlords:**
- Property details (location, rent, amenities)
- Property photos
- Next of kin information (name, phone, relationship)
- Emergency contact information

**For Fundis (Service Providers):**
- Service category (plumber, electrician, etc.)
- Tools owned
- Experience and skills
- Service area
- ID verification information (through CONEKTA Trust)

**For Identity Verification (CONEKTA Trust):**
- National ID number
- ID card photo
- Selfie photo (for verification)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- WhatsApp messages and conversation history with Mama Dennis
- Usage data (features used, search queries)
- Device information (phone model, operating system)
- Timestamps of interactions

### 2.3 Payment Information

- M-Pesa phone number (for contact reveal payments)
- Transaction records
- Payment timestamps

**Note:** We do NOT store M-Pesa PIN or other sensitive payment credentials.

## 4. HOW WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION

We take data security seriously and implement multiple layers of protection:

### 4.1 Encryption
- **All sensitive data is encrypted** using AES-256 encryption
- Phone numbers are encrypted in our database
- ID numbers are encrypted
- Next of kin information is encrypted
- M-Pesa phone numbers are encrypted

### 4.2 Access Controls
- Limited access to personal data (need-to-know basis)
- Admin authentication required
- Role-based access control

### 4.3 Technical Security
- HTTPS/TLS encryption for data in transit
- Secure API endpoints
- Rate limiting to prevent attacks
- Regular security audits
- Secure file storage for photos

### 4.4 Physical Security
- Database hosted on secure servers
- Regular backups (encrypted)
- Disaster recovery procedures
